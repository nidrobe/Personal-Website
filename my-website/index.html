    About Me

    Hi, I'm Nico, and I began my journey in technology with an apprenticeship as an Electronics Technician for Automation Technology. I later pursued a degree in Mechatronic Engineering, where I discovered my passion for software engineering. This passion developed during small university projects, such as developing software to record and analyze electrocardiogram (ECG) signals using a small hardware platform.

    These experiences sparked my interest in creating innovative software solutions, which led me to a Data & AI internship at an German IT project center. There, I deepened my knowledge and practical experience in collaborative software development. My growing interest in AI led me to complete my thesis, where I developed an AI-based energy and dynamics prediction system for electric vehicles.

    Currently, I'm traveling around the world, exploring new cultures and perspectives. This journey not only enriches my personal life but also inspires my professional growth as I seek to integrate diverse ideas into my work.
