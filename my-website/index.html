    About Me

    Hi, I'm Nico, and I began my journey in technology with an apprenticeship as an Electronics Technician for Automation Technology. I later pursued a degree in Mechatronic Engineering, where I discovered my passion for software engineering. This passion developed during small university projects.

    These projects sparked my interest in creating innovative software solutions, which led me to an internship within the IT-industry. There, I deepened my knowledge and practical experience in collaborative software development. With my growing interest in AI, I decided to complete my thesis within the same field, where I developed an AI-based energy and dynamics prediction system for electric vehicles.

    After my graduation I decided to travel around the world while constantely learning new technologies. This journey not only enriches my personal life but also inspires my professional growth. At the moment, I am particularly interested in cloud computing.
